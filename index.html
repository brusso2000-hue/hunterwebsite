Applications

Where Our Instruments
Get to Work

From aerospace repair to fine jewelry — Hunter Products systems are deployed wherever precision surface treatment matters.

Aerospace & Defense

Component Repair & Corrosion Control

MRO facilities use plating pens to restore worn or corroded surfaces on aircraft components without full disassembly. Selective nickel or gold plating restores conductivity and corrosion resistance on electrical connectors and fasteners.

Medical & Dental

Instrument Hardening & Restoration

Surgeons and dentists rely on Hunter's Carbitron system for tungsten carbide coating of cutting instruments. The diamond dusting technique — invented by Hunter Products — gives edges greater hardness and longevity than any alternative.

Tungsten CarbideDiamond DustingInstrument Repair
Jewelry & Restoration

Gold, Silver & Rhodium Plating

Jewelers and restoration specialists use the Micro-Metallizer for precision spot plating of rings, clasps, and antique pieces. Switch between 14K, 18K, 24K gold, silver, and rhodium without a full tank system.

Gold PlatingRhodiumAntique Restoration
Electronics & PCB Manufacturing

Precision Contact Plating

Electronics manufacturers plate gold and tin onto PCB contacts, connector pins, and edge connectors. The Micro-Metallizer's pen-tip precision avoids bridging and contamination on tight board layouts.

Hunter Products Inc. has been at the forefront of portable electroplating technology since 1970. Based in New Jersey, we develop and manufacture the instruments that professionals reach for when precision, portability, and reliability cannot be compromised.

"Our flagship innovation — diamond dusting — was developed for the medical field to give surgical instruments a harder, more precise cutting edge."

We don't make mass-market products. We make specialized instruments for people who know exactly what they're doing — and need equipment that keeps up. Every product we ship carries the engineering experience of over five decades in the trade.
